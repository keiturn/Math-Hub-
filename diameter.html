Learning Corner

Diameter Facts & Tips

Understand important concepts about circles and diameter measurements.

Diameter is Twice the Radius

The diameter of any circle is always two times longer than its radius.

Passes Through Center

A diameter must always pass through the exact center point of a circle.

Use Correct Units

Always use the same unit when measuring radius, area, or circumference.
