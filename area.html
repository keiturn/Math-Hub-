Geometry Tools

Select a geometric figure below and enter the required measurements to calculate its area quickly and accurately.

Rectangle

Area = Length × Width

Square

Area = Side²

Triangle

Area = ½ × Base × Height

Circle

Area = πr²

Parallelogram

Area = Base × Height

Trapezoid

Area = ((Base₁ + Base₂) × Height) ÷ 2

Rhombus

Area = (Diagonal₁ × Diagonal₂) ÷ 2

Kite

Area = (Diagonal₁ × Diagonal₂) ÷ 2
